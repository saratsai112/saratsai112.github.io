  • Aloha! My name is Sara, and I am currently a freshman at UC Berkeley intending to study Business Administration and Interdisciplinary Studies! In high school, I was the tiny but mighty drum major of the school marching band.

    Why the name tinypenguinpie?

    I have always loved penguins, so when I was creating my snapchat name I wanted to incorporate it somewhere in there. Since I am tiny, I figured tinypenguin would be a good name, but that was taken, so I just made up tinypenguinpie.

    Who drew that beautiful cover photo with the pie flying away from a distressed penguin?

    Why, thank you for noticing! I drew it on Illustrator, and am very proud of it because I had no idea how to use the program.

    Where are you from?

    At Berkeley, I live in Stern Hall (AKA the all-girls dorm in an old building on top of a very hard-to-climb hill nestled at the corner of campus). My hometown is Fremont, CA!
